Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients
•12 ounces of Spaghetti
•A few tablespoons of salt
•2 large eggs and 2 egg yolks
•1 ounce grated pecorino Romano
•1 ounce of grated Parmesan cheese
•3 ounces of Guanciale (bacon or ham) diced into 1/4 inch cubes

    Steps

  • Step 1: Place a large pot of water on the stove and add salt to it.


  • Step 2: Get a separate bowl and whisk the eggs and add salt, black pepper, and some pecorino to it.


  • Step 3: Let the water boil.


  • Step 4: Get a separate frying pan and cook the bacon/ham cubes with or without oil until crisp and dark.


  • Step 5: Now add the Spaghetti to the boiling water and keep it until it is firm or al-dente.


  • Step 6: Keep 1 cup of the pasta water and drain the pot of the rest of the water.


  • Step 7: Reheat the bacon/ham/guanciale on the frying pan if necessary.


  • Step 8: Serve some of the pasta in a serving bowl and add the egg-cheese-salt-pepper mixture and transfer the bacon/ham/guanciale from the skillet to the serving bowl.


  • Step 9: Gently stir.


    • Useful Tips

  • Add as much salt as preferred for your tastes.

  • Use more egg yolks and less egg whites for a creamier flavor.
